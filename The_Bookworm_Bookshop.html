About the Bookworm Bookshop

Sicne 1933, The Bookworm Bookshop has offered rare and hard-to-find titles for the discerning reader.
The Bookworm offers:

  • Friendly, knowledgeable, and corteous help
  • Free coffee and juice for our customers
  • A well-lit reading room so you can "try before you buy"
  • Four friendly cats: Esmeralda, Catherine, Dulcinea and Beatrice

Our hours are 10 A.M. to 9 P.M. weekdays, noon to 7 pm weekend.
